Recycling that we Accept:

Listed below are the types of recycling that we collect. From different plastics, cardboard, and glass, ensure that you are properly sorting the different materials.

How to Prepare the Recycling:

Recycling is a very misguided process. It requires more work than rinsing out your containers and drying them out before putting them to the curb. We need the recycling material to be in its purest form. No labels, adhesive, or caps are allowed to be on the plastic or else the batch will have the be relocated into the garbage process.
